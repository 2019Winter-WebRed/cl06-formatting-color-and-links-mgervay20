Stop and Smell the Roses!

All about roses and their different meanings!

Roses are one of the most popular flowers. They are used for weddings, proms, and other big events. Did you know that roses come in other colors aside from the classic reds, pinks, and white?

Many fun colors such as blue, purple, green, orange, or even multicolored, are obtained by florists by dying white roses. This is done by adding colored dye to the water where the roses are nourished by. These different colors often have meanings to them.

  • White Roses-purity, innocence, youth
  • Yellow Roses-friendship, joy
  • Peach Roses-gratitude, thankfulness
  • Pink Roses-femininity, elegance
  • Red Roses-passion, love, romance
  • Purple Roses-enchantment, wonder, mystery
  • Green Roses-rebirth, renewal, fertility
  • Blue Roses-unique, mysterious
  • Black Roses-death, mourning
